About

Devrent was founded in 2019 by a young Junior Developer that couldn't land a job because of all companies looking for developers with at least 5+ years of development. Unfortunately, the pandemic hit and the company had to shut it's doors. Now we are back, stronger then ever with a team of 12 designers, developers and architects with experiences from Juniors to Seniors. We love to teach and help each other grow, we strongly welcome newly graduated with a hunger to build future applications. We have ALL been new, been trough the struggle and the impostor syndrome.
